About dsmtech

When Lee Robinson started looking for a new role in Des Moines, he made his own list of local tech companies and job boards. It quickly became clear there was no single, reliable index for the area.

dsmtech began as a list of about 15 companies and has grown into a larger directory of public, private, and startup teams across Iowa. The goal is simple: help people discover great places to work without hunting across dozens of sites.

If you spot incorrect info or missing companies, please open an issue or a pull request.
